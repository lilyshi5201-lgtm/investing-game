# CEO Investing Game - Visual Strategy Edition

Take the helm of **Titan Holdings** (or your own custom firm) as the CEO and 100% owner. With **$5 billion** in starting cash, your goal is to grow your net worth while navigating a "synthetic but plausible" market engine that accounts for inflation, taxes, and global economic regimes.

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Complex Market Engine:** Stock prices are driven by macro shocks, sector-specific trends, dividends, and mean reversion.
* **Economic Regimes:** The market shifts between five states: **Boom, Expansion, Normal, Slowdown, and Crisis**, each affecting volatility and drift.
* **Employee Research System:** Hire staff to improve your analysis depth. Coverage ranges from "Basic" (1 employee) to "Institutional Desk" (40+ employees).
* **Automated Trading Plans:** Generate and execute high-conviction trading plans based on your team's research quality.
* **Financial Realism:** Manage a monthly payroll ($3,000/employee), pay a 10% corporate tax on profits, and combat a 10% annual inflation rate.
* **Interactive Visuals:** Dynamic charts for stock price history and company net worth (Nominal vs. Real).
* **Persistent Progress:** Automated saving to `ceo_investing_save.json` ensures your empire is preserved.
